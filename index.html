Case Study

LeadScout

A Python automation project built to help freelancers, web designers, agencies, and sales teams find businesses that may need websites or digital services.

LeadScout searches public OpenStreetMap business data and identifies local businesses with no listed website. The goal is to make outreach faster by giving freelancers and agencies a clean list of businesses they can verify and contact.

The tool searches by city and business category, filters businesses without websites, creates Google Maps and Google Search verification links, scores each lead, removes duplicate businesses, and exports the results to a clean CSV file.

In testing, LeadScout generated 1,711 South African business leads across Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria, and Sandton.
